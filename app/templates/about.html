About Us

We are a team of dedicated software engineering students from Haramaya University, passionate about linguistic diversity and technological innovation. Our project, the Afan Oromo Spell Checker, aims to address the challenges faced by writers and speakers of Afan Oromo in accurately communicating their thoughts and ideas.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals and communities by providing a reliable and accessible tool for improving written communication in Afan Oromo. By leveraging our expertise in software development and linguistics, we strive to enhance the quality and accuracy of Afan Oromo text, thereby promoting literacy and preserving cultural heritage.
